# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

This extension ("AI Influencer Studio") runs inside Airtable and may exchange data with external services that you configure (e.g., AI providers, storage, and a backend API). We only process data required to provide the features you enable.

## Data We Access
- Airtable base data that you explicitly connect (tables, fields, and records you select).
- Media you upload or generate through the extension.
- Configuration values you provide in the Setup tab.

## Data We Send
If you connect external services, the extension may send:
- Prompts and related metadata to AI providers you select.
- Media files to your configured storage provider or backend.
- Minimal identifiers needed to perform the requested operation.

## Data Storage
We do not store your Airtable data outside of Airtable unless you configure a backend or storage provider. Any external storage is controlled by your own accounts and settings.

## Security
All connections to external services should use HTTPS. API keys are stored in Airtable's extension settings and sent only to the services you configure.
